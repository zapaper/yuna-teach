## What this tells us

1. **High-frequency words (10+ hits)** are mostly common function words — they appear in EVERY passage regardless of topic. Drilling these specifically gives little marginal value; students already see them everywhere.

2. **Medium-frequency words (3-9 hits)** are the sweet spot — concrete vocabulary that PSLE actually tests in 语文应用 MCQ Q5-Q6 (vocabulary choice) and 短文填空. Examples: **保护、合理、鼓励、健康、制作、介绍、感受、反应、选择、其他**.

3. **Single-hit words** suggest one-shot appearances — useful as exposure but not drill priorities.

4. **High-coverage lessons** (those with the most tested words) likely contain the highest-density "general PSLE vocabulary" — those lessons matter more for PSLE prep than narrative-specific ones.

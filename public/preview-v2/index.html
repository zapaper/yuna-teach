FAQ

Common questions

What subjects and levels are covered?
Primary 4 to 6 English, Mathematics, Science and Chinese, all aligned with the latest MOE syllabus. Upper-primary content is driven by PSLE-grade material.
How does the personalised practice work?
After marking, Lumi reads your child's paper history and finds the recurring mistakes and weak sub-topics. It then builds personalised quizzes around those exact gaps, refreshed weekly. You don't pick what to assign — the next quiz is ready and waiting.
How does the AI marking work?
Students complete quizzes on their device or on printed worksheets. The AI reads handwriting, applies MOE scoring rubrics, awards partial marks part-by-part, and returns a result within minutes — with step-by-step explanations.
How do I submit my child's work?
Three ways: write directly on the in-app handwriting canvas, email a scanned paper to your child's print code, or use a printed worksheet. Each path routes the work back to the right student automatically.
How much does it cost?
MarkForYou is currently free during our beta period. Our priority is the best possible experience for families before we introduce pricing. No credit card required.
